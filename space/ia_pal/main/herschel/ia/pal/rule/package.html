This package defines rules to be used in to specify what may and may not be inserted into them. They are applied by overriding the {@link herschel.ia.pal.ListContext#getAddingRule()} or {@link herschel.ia.pal.MapContext#getAddingRule()}. Note that you can write your own rules or combine the ones here using predicates to make new and exciting ones. @see herschel.ia.pal.MapContext @see herschel.ia.pal.ListContext @see herschel.share.predicate
