6. Storage and PoolManagers for configured ProductStorages and ProductPools

It has been realized that site administrators (or other users) require a means of providing to their users pre-configured ProductStorage and ProductPool implementations without them needing to construct them 'manually'.

The design employed uses two managers: a StorageManager for ProductStorages, and a PoolManager for ProductPools. The administrator defines configuration files using either a Jython definition or an XML definition.
