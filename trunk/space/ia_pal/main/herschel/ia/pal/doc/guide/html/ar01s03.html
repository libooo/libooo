3. Introduction

This guide is intended:

  • To document the design of the PAL to aid developers of the PAL in future development and maintainence of the code.

  • To provide guidelines to developers using the PAL API, eg those implementing ProductPools (Section 6, “Storing products: ProductStorage and ProductPool”), and those who want to design complex persistent data structures for their users.

This document is intended to complement the information provided for the general user (astronomer or calibration scientist), in the DP User's Manual Chapter 12.2. It is strongly recommended to read that information first before reading this document.
